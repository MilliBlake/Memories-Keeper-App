Support

Need help with reminders, widgets, Apple Watch capture, sharing, or iCloud sync? Reach out and we will help you quickly.

support@escapeplan.ae

How do I capture fast?

Use Quick Add actions, app shortcut support, and widget or watch entry points for one-tap capture.

Can I browse by person?

Yes. People view organizes memories by person and lets you tap into full memory details instantly.

How does sharing work?

Share inside the app or through the iOS share sheet. Multi-select supports batch share/export actions.

Can I set reminder schedules?

Yes. Daily and weekly reminder notifications support day/time selection for flexible routines.

Is data private?

Memories are tied to your Apple ecosystem, stored locally, and synced via iCloud only when enabled.

Do you support legacy sharing?

Yes. Legacy contact and shared legacy support are available so memories can be preserved thoughtfully.
