Support

If you need help using Memories Keeper or want to share feedback, contact us any time and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

support@escapeplan.ae

How do I save a memory?

Open the app, tap to create a new memory, add your notes and optional media, then save.

Is my data private?

Yes. Memories are stored on your device and can sync through your own iCloud account if enabled.

Can I add photos and audio?

Yes. You can attach images and voice recordings to preserve more context with each entry.

Will the app keep improving?

Yes. We are continuing to ship quality-of-life improvements and new memory features over time.
