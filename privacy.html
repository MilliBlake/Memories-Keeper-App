Privacy Policy

Memories Keeper is designed with privacy at its core.

Last updated: March 2026

On-Device Storage

Your memories are stored locally on your device using Apple technologies designed for secure local data storage.

Encrypted iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud, your data may be synchronized through your personal iCloud account. Apple handles this sync using its own security and encryption systems.

No Third-Party Servers

Memories Keeper does not operate third-party servers to store your memories. We do not collect, store, or access your personal memory content outside of Apple’s ecosystem.

Photos Stay Private

Photos you attach to memories remain associated with your data on your device and, if enabled, your personal iCloud storage. They are not uploaded to external third-party services by Memories Keeper.

Audio Recordings

Voice recordings created in the app are stored with your data locally on your device and may sync through your iCloud account if enabled.

Location Data

Location is only saved when you choose to add it to a memory. Memories Keeper does not track your location in the background.

Data Sharing

Memories Keeper does not sell your data, does not share your personal memories with advertisers, and does not use your content for third-party marketing.

Children’s Privacy

Memories Keeper is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact support@escapeplan.ae.
