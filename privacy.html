Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Memories Keeper ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Memories Keeper is designed to store personal memories created by you. The content you create in the app, such as notes or memories, is stored for your personal use.

The app may also collect limited technical information necessary for the app to function properly, such as device type or app performance data.

How Your Information Is Used

Information stored in the app is used only to provide the core functionality of the application, allowing you to record and view your memories.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal content with third parties.

Data Storage

Your memories are stored within the app environment and are intended for your personal use only.

Third-Party Services

The app may use standard platform services provided by the operating system or app store to operate and improve performance.

Children’s Privacy

Memories Keeper is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
