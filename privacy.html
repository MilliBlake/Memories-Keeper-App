Privacy Policy

Memory Keeper is privacy-first by design. Your memories are tied to your Apple ecosystem and remain under your control.

Last updated: March 2026

Local-first storage

Memories are stored locally on your device for core use, without requiring third-party memory hosting.

iCloud sync when enabled

If you turn on iCloud sync, your content is synchronized through your personal Apple account using Apple's security systems.

User-controlled sharing

Memories are only shared when you choose to share in-app or through the iOS share sheet.

Media and metadata

Photos, voice notes, mood tags, people, and optional location details remain attached to your memory data and are not sold for advertising.

No advertising profile built from memories

We do not sell your memory content and do not use personal entries to build third-party advertising profiles.

Legacy support

Legacy contact and shared legacy features are designed to preserve memories responsibly based on your account settings.

Children's privacy

Memory Keeper is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy updates

Policy changes are published on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@escapeplan.ae.
