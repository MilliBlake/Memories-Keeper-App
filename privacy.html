Privacy Policy

Memories Keeper is built to keep your memories personal, private, and under your control.

Last updated: March 2026

On-device storage

Your memory content is stored locally on your device using Apple-supported storage technologies.

Encrypted iCloud sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synchronized through your personal iCloud account using Apple's security systems.

No third-party memory servers

Memories Keeper does not run third-party servers for storing your entries, photos, or recordings.

Photos and audio

Photos and voice recordings you add remain associated with your app data on-device and in your iCloud account if sync is enabled.

Location data

Location is saved only when you actively choose to attach it to a memory. The app does not track background location.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data, use your memory content for advertising, or share personal entries for third-party marketing.

Children's privacy

Memories Keeper is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy over time. Revisions are posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@escapeplan.ae.
