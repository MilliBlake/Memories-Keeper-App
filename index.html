Core features built for real life

From quick notes to rich moments, Memories Keeper is designed for speed, context, and long-term recall without sacrificing privacy or simplicity.

Fast capture flow

Use Quick Add actions, app shortcuts, and one-tap entry to save text, photos, mood, and optional voice notes in seconds.

Rich memory details

Open full-screen photo galleries, attach people and location, and keep each moment complete and easy to revisit.

Timeline + People views

Browse memories by date in Timeline or by person in People view, with shared/friend memory tap-through into full details.

Available everywhere

Stay in flow with iPhone, Apple Watch, and widget support, plus daily or weekly reminders with flexible day/time selection.

Private by default

Data is tied to your Apple ecosystem, stored locally, and synced through iCloud when enabled. Sharing is always user-controlled.

Batch share and export

Multi-select memories for batch actions, then share in-app or through the standard iOS share sheet whenever you choose.
